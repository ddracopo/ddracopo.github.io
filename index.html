Personal Information

1990: Diploma in Electrical Engineering, National Technical University of Athens.

1994: PhD, Department of Computing, Imperial College, University of London.

1994-1998: Lecturer, Department of Computer Science, Brunel University, London. Visiting Associate Professor, Ecole Normale Superieure de Lyon, Laboratoire de l'Informatique du Parallelisme, Lyon, France (1996-1997).

1998-2004: Senior Software Engineer in various international companies.

Currently: Senior Lecturer, Department of Computer Science, University of Westminster, London. .

Research Interests

Neural networks, evolutionary computing, neurocontrol, intelligent and adaptive control, artificial life, bioinformatics, robotics, distributed computing, web-based computing, computer vision, image processing.
